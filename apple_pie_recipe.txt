# Apple Pie Recipe

## Ingredients:
- 6 cups sliced apples (Granny Smith or similar)
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter (cold, diced)
- 1 package (2 1/2 cups) all-purpose flour (for crust)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup cold water
- 1 egg (for egg wash)

## Instructions:
1. **Prepare the crust**: In a bowl, mix 2 1/2 cups flour and salt. Cut in cold butter until crumbly. Add water gradually to form dough. Chill for 30 minutes.
2. **Roll out the crust**: Roll one half of the dough into a circle and place it in a 9-inch pie dish. Trim edges.
3. **Make the filling**: Toss sliced apples with sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Pile into the crust. Dot with cold butter.
4. **Top the pie**: Roll out the second dough half and place over the filling. Crimp edges and cut slits for steam. Brush with egg wash.
5. **Bake**: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake for 50-60 minutes until golden brown. Cool before serving.
